Overview

Purpose

I have been exploring the medium of writing to express myself more, but also I just really enjoy it regardless of expression. This website will be a portfolio of all my different writings that I can keep updated. The website will serve as a journal or a time capsule, as I progress in and pursue the world of writing. I'll have it separated into Poetry, Creative Non-Fiction, and Fiction.

Audience

The audience of this site will be my family, friends, peers, and hopefully any future fans of my writing.

Dynamic elements

  • Interactive Navigation: Smooth scrolling, dropdown menus, and animated transitions for better navigation.
  • Dynamic Content Loading: Loading additional content without refreshing the page, such as infinite scrolling or "Load More" buttons.
  • Animated Effects: Subtle animations for buttons, hover effects, and other interactive elements to make the site more engaging.
  • Dark Mode: The option to switch between light and dark modes to ease the burden on reader's eyes.
