Poetry

Eowyn

It’s hard to notice when she’s there,
That unwelcome visitor.
Her intrusion in my life
Is a constant ruination of my day.
She haunts and taunts from the corner of my eye.
Like a reminder of my own desperation,
She calls for my attention despite knowing
I’d never want it to be hers.
The cowardice of her being,
Never fully facing the world,
Content to wallow in her sallow pallor.
The scars she leaves on this world are temporary,
But you will find them everywhere,
In every deep recess and niche,
Every cranny and corner,
The evidence of her presence
is inescapable.
There when you leave,
Awaiting your return.
The sanctuary you once held sure,
Tainted and tarnished,
A shadow of what once was.
The only thing she offers me
Is the conviction,
That I’ll never own a cat.

Look on my Works, ye Mighty, and despair!

Out there, there’s a plane, where remembrance fades and the
Zeitgeist of the world has left you forgotten in the gutter.
Your kingdom once stretched far, into distant horizons.
Mighty was your roster, now faded from the wind and sands.
Almost grasping godhood, your reach could not extend.
Ne’er closer to the sun, than the day you were born icarus.
Do not think that good night offers anything but gent.
If words were your weapon, rust did away with them
A long time ago. Nevertheless, being forgotten is a treasure.
Solace, a gift. And yours, the greatest of all.

Stagnant Silkworm

Spin your little lies, and weave your tall tales.
It’s through the distortions of your wake that
The truth of all things becomes visible.
Honesty never was your friend, and so you pushed her away,
Further than she’d be, had you asked for her name.
The things you care to hide, unseen as they are,
Tell much about the truths you bury deep inside.
With no sleeve to wear your heart on, it’s easy to see the scars.
The pain that knows you well, can only fade, but never die.
Stare long and hard, the abyss grows tired of your gaze.
You can look but never touch, but what good are the senses,
When everything else has betrayed you so far?
I know the red you wear is the same shade as mine,
But the lies I tell myself are beginning to flicker,
Soon they’ll be no more. With the dying of that light,
I’ll emerge a new being, that warm cocoon offers no comfort now.
So let those chains go, the ones you hold oh so tight.
I think we both know that they’ve been loose for a while.

Opportunity

I’d like to think that i would love the chance to cheat,
to break a heart. To cause some pain from popularity,
and have all i could surrounding me. Too stressed to decide
cause my options are many. But i wake up and know that that isn’t my life.
I cannot be yours cause you just don’t have time.
I can’t break any hearts cause they always break mine.

Sunsets

I can’t see you in sunsets,
Cause Those we never shared,
You’d only call me after dark
On nights you didn’t care

Pretend that i don’t see the signs
Make do like we’re complete
Ignore the voice from everyone
That’s telling me to leave

And if we could be whole again
she’d say we never were
It’s far too late to lose my love
But not too late for her

haven’t had reason nor rhyme
Wish things that i must not
all we shared was borrowed time
And i hoped god forgot

It’s only when I think of you
That heaven starts to sigh
If words suffice to describe my plight
I’d think that pigs would fly
