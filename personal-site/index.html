About the author

Corb bounced around, meandered, drifted, journeyed, wandered, and wondered throughout his life. Claiming Arizona as his home state, he spent most of his pubescent years going to school in Peoria, AZ. Whether or not his teachers believed in him is a non-factor, because writing only became an interest when he chose to write weekly emails home serving a mission in Argentina. Met with praise for his prose, he realized this was a nobler pursuit to engage in. He is currently a sophomore in College, minoring in Creative Writing, but he has high hopes that the worlds he spends time creating take him far.
